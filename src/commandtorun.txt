Has to be done in - x64 Native Tools Command Prompt for VS 

after the new bat files -

cd D:\pq-blockchain
build_cryptotest.bat
crypto_test.exe

cd D:\pq-blockchain
build_blockchain.bat
crypto_blockchain.exe


But if we add new files, edit the commands below and use them 
for testing crypto without blockchain -

cl /EHsc /std:c++17 ^
  src\main_crypto_test.cpp ^
  src\algo_config.cpp ^
  src\crypto_factory.cpp ^
  src\oqs_mldsa_crypto.cpp ^
  src\oqs_falcon_crypto.cpp ^
  src\hawk_crypto.cpp ^
  src\timing.cpp ^
  D:\oqs-hawk\dev\Optimized_Implementation\avx2\*.c ^
  /ID:\pq-blockchain\include ^
  /ID:\liboqs\build\include ^
  /ID:\oqs-hawk\dev\Optimized_Implementation\avx2 ^
  /link /LIBPATH:D:\liboqs\build\lib\Release oqs.lib ^
        /out:crypto_test.exe

  src\timing.cpp ^
  src\block_utils.cpp ^
  src\transaction.cpp ^
  src\block.cpp ^
  src\blockchain.cpp ^
  src\wallet.cpp ^
  D:\oqs-hawk\dev\Optimized_Implementation\avx2\*.c ^
  /ID:\pq-blockchain\include ^
  /ID:\liboqs\build\include ^
  /ID:\oqs-hawk\dev\Optimized_Implementation\avx2 ^
